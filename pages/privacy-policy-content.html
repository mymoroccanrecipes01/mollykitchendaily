Privacy Notice

Welcome to Molly Kitchen Daily

At Molly Kitchen Daily, we're committed to providing simple and practical recipe ideas while respecting your privacy. Since we don't require user accounts, we avoid collecting or handling your personal information directly. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

However, certain information may be collected automatically through external services that help us deliver a better experience—from improved site performance to personalized advertisements and engaging video content. This notice explains how these practices work and the steps we take to protect your privacy while you enjoy mollykitchendaily.com.

Third-Party Tools and Services

To keep Molly Kitchen Daily free and running smoothly, we rely on several external platforms. These services help us analyze site metrics, generate revenue through advertising, and provide interactive video content. Here are the key third-party services we use:

Google Analytics: This service provides valuable visitor insights by anonymously tracking user interactions (such as pages visited and time spent on site). It uses cookies but doesn't give Molly Kitchen Daily access to personal data. These insights help us improve our content to better meet your needs.

Google AdSense: This advertising platform displays ads on our site to fund our free content. It uses cookie-based browsing patterns to show personalized advertisements. Molly Kitchen Daily cannot access any personal data that Google may use for ad targeting. For more details, please review Google's privacy policy.

Cloudflare Analytics: Cloudflare helps us by tracking technical data like traffic performance and browser types, without using cookies for individual identification. This aggregated information helps us optimize mollykitchendaily.com and maintain security.

YouTube: We occasionally embed recipe videos hosted on YouTube (a Google platform). When you watch these videos, YouTube or Google may place cookies that collect viewing statistics, such as view counts. If you're signed into a Google account, YouTube may link this activity to your profile. Molly Kitchen Daily doesn't have access to this collected data. Visit YouTube's privacy policy for additional information.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit websites. Molly Kitchen Daily and our trusted partners use cookies to enhance your browsing experience:

Functional Cookies: These are essential for basic site functionality, such as remembering your settings or consent preferences. Disabling them may affect some site features.

Analytics Cookies: Services like Cloudflare Analytics and Google Analytics use these to understand visitor trends, helping us improve mollykitchendaily.com's content and design.

Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense uses these cookies to display ads tailored to your interests and browsing behavior.

By continuing to use mollykitchendaily.com, you consent to the use of these cookies. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, receive alerts before they're stored, or clear them manually. However, disabling cookies may negatively impact your experience on our site and others.
